ABOUT US

We practice protect animal species and their habitats. As part of the world’s ecosystems, Paws and Claws provides balance and stability to nature’s processes, our goal conservation is to ensure the survival of these species, and to educate people on living sustainably with other species. Developing and nurturing an appreciation of nature in our people is a fundamental element that safeguards the safety of our wildlife and environment,if we can get our people involved by teaching them the value of wildlife conservation.
